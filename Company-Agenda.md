## 3) Company Productivity Framework

### A) Weekly Planning (every Monday)
1. Pick top **3 outcomes** for the week.
2. Break each outcome into concrete tasks.
3. Assign owner + due date.
4. Track blockers daily.

### B) Daily Execution (15-minute routine)
- Update this board:
  - Done yesterday
  - Doing today
  - Blocked by
- Keep focus on P0/P1 first.

### C) End-of-Week Review
- What was shipped?
- What slipped and why?
- What should be automated/delegated next week?
